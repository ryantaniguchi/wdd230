Amenities

We offer a number of free and low-cost amenities to all of our guests. Each stay includes the following:

  • free continental breakfast
  • exercise and workout rooms
  • indoor pool
  • WiFi high-speed Internet access
  • smart televisions in each suite
  • on-site parking
  • complimentary temple shuttle access
  • family history consultation and family ordinance card service

Missionary Services

At Temple Inn & Suites, we fully support the important work that full-time missionaries do. We provide family search services as well as babysitting and other services meant to help make it easier for full-time missionaries to conduct their important work.

If you are a full-time missionary, we are happy to provide whatever assistance we can. We provide many services exclusively to members of the Church conducting work as full-time missionaries.

Find Out more on our Missionary Page

Reception Services

At Temple Inn & Suites, we are happy to help you celebrate your happy occasions. We offer a number of specialty services, including temple-themed receptions and catering services.

In addition to many of the reception and event services traditionally offered by hotels and inns, we offer a number of specialty servies that can only be provided by Temple Inn & Suites locations.
