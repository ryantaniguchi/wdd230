Event Accomodations

Temple Inn & Suites has a long history of providing event services for our guests. We are happy to help you plan for every occasion, including weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and other occasions. We have a number of services that will help you to celebrate your special day, including a wedding reception hall that we will happily help to customize with a catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and service.

Contact us for more information

Food Services

Each Temple Inn & Suites location offers the following food and drink services:

  • free continental breakfast
  • in-house restaurant serving local foods and drinks
  • food stations throughout each location offering food services
  • a close working relationship with local food and drink services
  • catering services for special events

Setup

Our friendly and helpful staff are happy to assist with any preparations that your big day may require, including:

  • storage for event equipment and food
  • on-location parking services
  • help with managing and directing guests
  • help with setting up and removing decorations and equipment
