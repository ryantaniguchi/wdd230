Event Accomodations

Temple Inn & Suites has a long history of providing event services for our guests. We are happy to help you plan for every occasion, including weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and other occasions. We have a number of services that will help you to celebrate your special day, including a wedding reception hall that we will happily help to customize with a catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and service.

Food Services

Each Temple Inn & Suites location offers free continental breakfast, as well as access to an in-house restaurant serving local foods and drinks. We have stations throughout each Temple Inn & Suites services where food services can be offered, and each of our locations works closely with local food and drink vendors, providing additional options for catering and other food services.

Setup

Our friendly and helpful staff are happy to assist with any preparations that your big day may require, including providing storage, coordinating parking, helping to direct guests, and helping with setting up and removing decorations and equipment.
