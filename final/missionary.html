Full-Time Missionaries

Temple Inn & Suites fully supports the work done by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, both in their humanitarian efforts and in their spiritual work to help our brothers and sisters, both living and deceased.

Contact us for more information

Supporting Our Full-Time Missionaries

Each of our locations provides services supporting our hard-working fultime missionary guests. These include:

  • free access to changing rooms
  • long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries
  • bathroom facilties for temple patrons who may need to prepare for or have recently finished a visit to the temple

Providing for Temple Visitors

Please take advantage of our hotels' many accommodations designed to help our full-time missionary guests:

  • playroom for younger guests
  • low cost, short-term babysitting services
  • family search center that supports family search and ordinance work
  • temple-history themed reception and sitting areas
