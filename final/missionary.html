Full-Time Missionaries

Temple Inn & Suites fully supports the work done by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, both in their humanitarian efforts and in their spiritual work to help our brothers and sisters, both living and deceased.

Supporting Our Full-Time Missionaries

Each Temple Inn & Suites location provides free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilties for temple patrons who may need to prepare for or have recently finished a visit to the temple.

Each Temple Inn & Suites location has a family search center that supports family search and ordinance work, allowing our guests to conduct their important work from the comfort of one of our many locations.

Providing for Temple Visitors

Each Temple Inn & Suites location has a playroom available for guests, as well as short-term babysitting services for our younger guests.

Each Temple Inn & Suites location has at least one beautifully decorated temple-history themed reception and sitting area for the use of our guests.
