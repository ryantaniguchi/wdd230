The Colorado Kid was commissioned in 1907 to commemorate Black Forest's rich cowboy history, including famous names such as Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid who once called Black Forest home.

Black Forest has a strong commercial sector, and is home to many of Colorado's Fortune 500 companies. 43% of Black Forest University graduates finish school with a degree in Business or a related field.

Come join us everyon May 13th for Black Forest Days, an annual celebration commemorating Black Forest's rich history. There will be food, games, and appearances from celebrities and musicians.

Visit Black Forest's original City Center, a historic part of town that has been in use since the town's founding in 1853. One popular site is the Ursine Academy, which was founded in 1872 to serve the learning needs of the community's youth. The tradition of education has continued into the current day, with a full 68% of Black Forest adults having some form of higher education.

Black Forest is home to a wide range of beautiful parks, including hte Jamon Iberico de Bellota Park in upper Black Forest. With an average population growth of 2%, Black Forest has plenty of resources catering to the needs of our young citizens.
