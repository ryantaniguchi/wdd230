Membership Level
Become an associate member of the Black River Chamber of Commerce at no cost. Join the Bronze Membership level of the Chamber of Commerce for $50/month. This grants you access to view recorded and live meetings of the Black Forest Chamber of Commerce, access to newsletters, and event discounts. Join the Silver Membership level of the Chamber of Commerce for $100/month. Beyond Bronze Member perks, this grants you access to special trainings and seminars. Additional discounts and special offers are also available. Join the Gold Membership level of the Chamber of Commerce for $200/month. Beyond Silver Member perks, Gold Members are granted voting rights in Chamber of Commerce decisions and featured on the Black Forest Chamber of Commerce website and resources.
